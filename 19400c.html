Castro Valley • VEX Robotics

Team 19400C — build, iterate, compete.

We’re a student-run team designing, building, and programming robots for the VEX Robotics Competition. Our mission is to grow as engineers, innovators, and leaders while representing our school and community.

About Team 19400C

Student-run • Hands-on engineering • Community impact

What we do

We design, build, and program competition robots, applying CAD, fabrication, control systems, and strategy to real events.

Why it matters

Students gain practical STEM skills, teamwork, leadership, and resilience—preparing for college and careers in engineering.

Where support goes

Donations directly fund robot parts, tools, competition fees, and travel—every dollar keeps us building and competing.

Our Team

Dedicated students & mentors

Mechanical

Design, CAD, fabrication, testing, and iteration for competitive performance.

Programming

Autonomous routines, driver control, sensors, and match strategy tools.

Operations

Outreach, fundraising, documentation, and sponsor relations.

Season Snapshot

Key milestones at a glance

Kickoff & Game Reveal Early season — rules, strategy, and prototyping begin
Build & Test Mid-season — iterate mechanisms, tune code, practice driving
Qualifiers & State Late season — compete, refine, and push for advancement
Community Outreach All year — demos, STEM nights, and mentoring younger students
